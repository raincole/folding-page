Folding

Goal

Turn the structure into a straight line or a loop.

Instruction

You can rotate a segment which has a handle(hollow circle) at its end. When it hits another segment, it stops if it's aligned to any axis(i.e. up, left, down, right), or it bounces back to previous position otherwise.

Only the outermost nodes will become handles.

Control

Mouse Clickselect
W, A, S, Drotate
